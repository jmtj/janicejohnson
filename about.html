Professional Path:

Those who know me well know that solving problems and making things better for people have always motivated me and brought me joy.

After years of taking jobs that came to me because of my previous experiences, I am changing gears to create new opportunities that really excite and energize me.

I know that asking inquiring questions and being able to pivot mindfully is very important in obtaining insights.

I love projects that allow me to combine all my skills and passions to develop solutions that can make a difference for users. If you have a project to share with me, I would love to connect!
